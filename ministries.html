Bethany AME Church Ministries

Bethany Community Center


Bethany Community Center provides a variety of church and community services.

  • Worship Services (Revivals and Bible Study)
  • Meetings
  • Banquets
  • Organized Sport Activities (Skate Around, Basketball, etc.)
  • Tutoring
  • Place to donate food items and clothing

Bible Study


Bible study classes are held for members and non-members to study, share, and discuss the Scriptures. We pray that all students will share the good news of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ with others.

Church School


On Sunday mornings, church school is available to provide quality teaching of the Bible and religious education in an environment where students can participate and learn.

Health Services


Health Services provide greater interest in health education and maintaining healthy minds and bodies.

Hospitality


The Hospitality Committee works to provide a friendly, healthy, and generous atmosphere for members, guests and others at all functions.

Lay Orginization


The Lay Organization strives to instill in the membership of the church a love and appreciation for the history, tradition, and principles of African Methodism.

Men Fellowship


The Men Fellowship encourages men to become involved in leading other men to the acceptance of Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior.

Missionary Society



Missionaries work diligently to serve and help others as they spread the good news of Jesus Christ.

Music Department



The Music Department provides music for the church and helps inspire others through songs.

Steward Board


The Stewards conduct the temporal business of the church and seek out the needy and distressed in order to relieve and comfort them.

Stewardesses


Stewardesses provide the elements for the holy communion and dress the pulpit and altar rail with suitable clean white converings.

Trustees


Trustees take care of the real estate, the church, parsonage and other property owned by the local church. They make improvements to the property or real estate when authorized.

Usher Board


Ushers greet and seat vistors and members, monitor the doors, issue materials and provide information when needed.

Women of Destiny


Women of Destiny work together and use their skills in Godly ways to help others grow spritually, positively and productively.

Young People's Division


The Young People's Division (YPD) provides meaningful youth training programs in all church areas. It helps youth develop interest in discovering Christian opportunities.
