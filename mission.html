OUR MISSION AND PURPOSE



The Mission of the African Methodist Episcopal Church is to minister to the spiritual, intellectual, physical and emotional needs of all people by spreading Christ's liberating Gospel through word and deed. Bethany African Methodist Episcopal Church shall engage in carrying out the spirit of the orginal Free African Society out of which the african Methodist Episcopal Church evolved.







The AME Motto

"God Our Father, Christ Our Redeemer, the Holy Spirit Our Comforter, Humankind Our Family."
